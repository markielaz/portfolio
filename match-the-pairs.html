'Match The Pairs' - a fun little memory game

Before we ventured into the world of React, we were mostly using Vanilla JS to build our projects. Here is an example of one of my earlier efforts, focusing on DOM manipulation

  • The game starts out with a series of paired images that are hidden from the user.
  • The first 2 images the user clicks on are revealed.
  • If the revealed images match they remain uncovered.
  • If the images do not match, they are hidden once again.
  • The user continues until they have matched all the images.
